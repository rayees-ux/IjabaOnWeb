About the Website

IjabaOnWeb is a modern digital platform created to provide clear and authentic answers to Islamic Fiqh questions, covering both classical and contemporary issues.

The platform aims to simplify complex jurisprudential topics and present them in a structured, user-friendly format, making it easier for students and learners to understand Islamic rulings.

By combining traditional Islamic scholarship with modern web design, IjabaOnWeb ensures that knowledge is not only accurate but also easily accessible in today’s digital world.

Darul Falah Islamic Academy

Darul Falah Islamic Academy is a meritorious educational institution affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University, located in Taliparamba, Kerala, India. It was established in September 2012 under the stewardship of the Taliparamba Yatheem Khana Committee.

The committee, which actively runs orphan care programs, Islamic madrassas, and various schools, recognized the gradual decline of Islamic educational heritage in Taliparamba. Darul Falah was founded as a response to this concern.

The institution aims to shape a generation equipped with both religious and worldly knowledge, enabling them to face modern challenges and contribute as responsible Islamic scholars and propagators.

Along with academic programs, the academy conducts extracurricular activities such as writing and speaking training to nurture students' talents. The active student body, SAAF (Students Association of Al Falah), provides various opportunities for student engagement and development.

The campus features a well-designed building with spacious and modern facilities, along with a large playground that enhances the overall environment.
